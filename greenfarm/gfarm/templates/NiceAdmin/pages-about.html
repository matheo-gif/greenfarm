About us

The Greenfarm Maize Crop Management Web Application system is the computer online system project that is intent in helping the greenfarm organization in tracing the history of the farmers and the records of their harvest. It will also help in guiding the farmers on how the crop is planted and which breed to be grown in their distinct region, location or specific area as advised by agriculturalists. The application will also be used in registration of new members and keep their records. It will also enable the availability of information about the crop and the organization.
